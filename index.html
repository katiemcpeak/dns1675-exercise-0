Beetroot (Beta Vulgaris)

55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest the root.

The usually deep purple roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled, or roasted as a crooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegetable.
